### Textbooks

_Reading in_: Panton, Ronald L. _Incompressible Flow_. 4th ed. Wiley, 2013. ISBN: 9781118013434. \[Preview with [Google Books](http://books.google.com/books?id=sa4eAAAAQBAJ&pg=PAfrontcover)\]
